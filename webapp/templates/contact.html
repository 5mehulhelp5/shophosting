Common Questions

Do you offer phone support?

We primarily offer support via email and chat. For complex issues, we're happy to schedule a call. Just let us know in your message.

Can you help migrate my existing store?

Yes! We offer free migration assistance for all new customers. We'll move your store with zero downtime. You just need to update your DNS when we're ready.

What are your business hours?

Our team is available Monday through Friday, 9am to 6pm Eastern Time. However, our monitoring systems watch your store 24/7.

I'm not sure which plan I need. Can you help?

Absolutely. Book a free consultation and we'll review your store's needs together. We'll recommend the right plan based on your traffic, catalog size, and growth plans.
